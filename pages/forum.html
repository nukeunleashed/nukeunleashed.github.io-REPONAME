The whole idea that "Mockingjays show that the Capitol made a mistake" is more a District thing, which only really became promoted during the Second Rebellion. Mockingjays are so common around Panem that many in the Capitol like them, and therefore seeing them in arenas doesn't really make the Capitol look bad (at least not to people in the Capitol). Think of how many Capitol people started wearing Mockingjay inspired outfits, until Snow had to ban the practice once the Rebellion really started becoming a threat.
